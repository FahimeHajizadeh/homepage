About Me

I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University, Tehran, Iran, in 2015. Then, I started my master's studies at Kharazmi University in Computer Engineering (Hardware Architecture) and I received my M.Sc. degree in 2019. I've joined Polytechnique Montréal, Electrical Engineering department in Jan. 2022 as a Ph.D. student.


My research interests are Real-Time Systems, VLSI Systems Approximate Computing, Computer Architecture, Electronic Circuits and Devices, and Hardware Acceleration. Now, I'm working on Real-time systems over FPGA.
